    Our Ciders

    Old Cider House is Colorado's first cidery since Prohibition, bringing natrual flavors of Colorados finest apples across the globe. Our goal is to bridge the gap between wine and beer with flavorful, small-batch cider, Old Cider Houses' offers - Dry and Semi-Sweet, breaking the mold of overly sweet ciders. Old Cider House products are handcrafted with natural ingredients.

    Cider Made Simple

    Made from a custom blend of fresh pressed, locally grown Colorado apples, Old Cider House's products are handcrafted with real ingredients, distinguishing themselves from the mass market cider found in stores today.

    • Apples

      A custom blend of fresh pressed Colorado grown apples are used to create our cider.

    • Yeast and Alcohol

      Fruit and yeast are used to achieve Old Cider's unique flavor.

    • Brown Sugar

      Brown cane sugar is used to achieve the subtle sweetness found in our ciders.

    • Fruits and Spices

      Natrual Ingredients such as cinnammon, pumpkin, herbs and ripe fruits are used during fermentation to create unique seasonal ciders.
